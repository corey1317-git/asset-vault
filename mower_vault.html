Welcome to Mower Vault

Project repair checklist & flips tracker

Mower Vault is a digital ledger designed to help track riding lawn mowers you buy, repair, maintain, and sell. It is completely private and runs 100% on your device.

100% Private & Offline Storage

No mowers, serials, costs, or repair logs are ever uploaded to Thorpe Tech servers. Everything is stored inside your browser's local cache database.

⚠️ CRITICAL BACKUP WARNING: Clearing your browser history, cookies, or database cache will erase all your projects. Make sure to use the Save Backup or Google Drive Save buttons regularly to keep a copy of your database.

What the App Does

🔧 Specifications Filler Get Deck Belts, Drive Belts, Spark Plugs, and Oil capacities automatically using the **AI Specs Enrich** tool.
📊 Flip Business Ledger Type in your buying price, repair costs, and final sale price. The app handles margins and ROI calculations.
🛠 Maintenance & Parts Log Keep track of standard maintenance checkmarks (oil, carburetor, sharpening) and log individual part costs.
📺 DIY Repair Videos Instant shortcuts to YouTube belt routings, mandrel changes, and carburetor fixes specific to your mower model.

How to Get Started

  1. Add a mower via the + Add Mower button on the left.
  2. Open the mower, select **Specs & Parts**, and click **AI Specs Enrich** to search for belt parts.
  3. Add parts expenses in the **Repair Log** tab; this will automatically calculate total flip expenses.
  4. Change the status to **Flipped / Sold** and enter the final sale price in the **Business Ledger** to log your profit margin!
