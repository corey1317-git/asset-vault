What to do in an Accident
  • Safety First: Pull over to a safe area, turn on hazard lights, and check for injuries. Call 911 immediately if emergency care is required.
  • Swap Information: Share your name, contact phone, and this digital Insurance policy with other drivers involved.
  • Document Scene: Take photos of all vehicle damages, license plates, the accident intersection, and the other driver's license card.
  • File a Police Report: In many jurisdictions, calling the police is required to create a formal collision record for insurance.
