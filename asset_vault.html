Welcome to Asset Vault!

Your secure, local-first inventory and warranty manager.

Think of this app as a digital filing cabinet for your home. You can add things you own (like appliances, lawn mowers, or electronics), save their serial numbers, upload photos of receipts, and keep track of when their warranties end so you never lose out on a repair claim.

1
100% Private (On Your Computer) Everything you enter is stored directly on your own device. No user accounts, no passwords, and no data is ever sent to our servers.
2
Save Backups regularly (Crucial) Since data sits in your browser, clearing your history will erase it. We highly recommend using the Google Drive Save button at the top to save a safe copy. To load your inventory back on any device, simply click Import and select your saved backup file.
3
Optional AI features (Free Key) Using the AI scanner to read receipts is completely optional. If you wish to use it, you can obtain a free API key from Google. If not, you can easily type everything in manually.
4
Warranty Expiry Notifications The app monitors your dates and flags warranties: Green (Active), Amber (Expiring within 90 days), and Red (Expired) so you never miss a coverage window.
